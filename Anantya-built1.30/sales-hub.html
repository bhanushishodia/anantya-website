2. Hassle-Free Client Onboarding

3. Comprehensive Overview: Onboarding Process


Once the onboarding team receives the email and sales form from the salesperson, they will kickstart the process. The initial onboarding process email will be sent to the client to introduce them to the process, followed by an onboarding meeting. Let's get this show on the road!

4. Onboarding Meeting
